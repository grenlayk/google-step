Stories

There are some world-wide events that I would like to describe.

World Robot Olympiad

Every year host country provides participants with new challenges with different difficulty according to their age and experience. Participants got almost a year to solve these challenges and to get to the top of teams in their own country. Most teams use LEGO to complete the tasks.

I took part in international finals 6 times in total and visited Indonesia, Qatar, India, Costa Rica, Thailand, Philippines, China. They are amazing places! Just take a look:

RoboCup

Main goal of this event is to make it possible for robots to win World Cup Football champions by the middle of 21th century. This event is mostly for adults, real engineers. There is no place for constructor, you need to design and make most of parts of your robot by yourself.
